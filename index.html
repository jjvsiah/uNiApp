About

At u N i, we believe that university is more than just lectures and exams—it's about building meaningful connections that enrich your campus life. Our app is designed to encourage real-life interactions by pairing you with fellow students who share your interests. Whether you’re looking to make new friends, find study buddies, or join a group with shared passions, u N i makes it easy to connect and meet up in person.

Our unique pairing system matches you in pairs or small groups based on your preferences and interests, ensuring that every meet-up is engaging and relevant. With u N i, you have a limited time to connect in real life, making each interaction purposeful and memorable.
